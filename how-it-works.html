System overview

The system is designed around predictable, controlled intersection behavior. The intent is to support emergency response movement while reducing surprise and conflict for the public.

Engage

Activation occurs early enough to allow a safe transition at the signal.

Clear

Signals progress through a controlled pattern intended to reduce conflict points.

Return

After the event, signals return to normal operation with minimal disruption.
